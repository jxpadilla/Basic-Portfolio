About Me

My background is in business and business computer system implementations. I have a bachelors degree in accounting and an MBA. During my career I've been an accountant, management accountant, controller, financial analyst, customer support consultant, and software consultant at customer sites.
Most of my professional experience has been with SAP, a very large software development company for business applications primarily in the ERP and business intelligence market. I have been described as a diligent problem solver that is an excellent team player that can also fly solo.
My passion is to empathize with customer needs to deliver lasting results. On the personal side, I like to work out, tend to my herb garden and am an accomplished home cook, according to my vegan wife ;)
